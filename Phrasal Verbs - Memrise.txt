Phrasal Verbs - Memrise

Bring up: looking after a child until he / she is grown up
Cover up (a crime or a wrongful action): hide the truth
Come to: regain consciousness
Catch up (with): succeed in finding someone, get up-to-date with news
Cope with: deal with, manage
Check in: register
Cut down: reduce
Come down: become ill with a specific illness
Come across: meet, find
Do away with: cancel / get rid of
Come up agaisnt: find something unexpectedly difficult
Cool off: lower the heat / calm down
