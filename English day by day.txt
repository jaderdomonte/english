Inglês do dia a dia

1. 	How are you? 		(É muito usada)
	How's it going?
	How are you today?
	What's up?
	What are you up to?

2.	I'm fine, thanks. 						And you? (Mais usada em situações formais)
	I'm doing good.							What about you?
	I'm doing ok.							How are you doing?
	I'm doing great! Thanks for asking.
	I'm well!
	I'm good.
	I'm great!
	Not so good!
	So so!

3.	You're welcome.	(Mais usada em situações formais)
	No problem. 	(Pode parecer grosseiro)
	Happy to help you.
	Happy to help.
	Don't mention it.
	No biggie! (No big deal.)
	My pleasure.
	Anytime.

4.	My name is... 	(Mais usada em situações formais ou quando conhece alguém pela primeira vez)
	I'm...			(Mais usada no dia a dia)
	Hi, ....

5.	Pardon me? / I beg your pardon? (Extremamente formal)
	Excuse me?
	Can you repeat that, please?
	I'm sorry.

6.	So so.			(Está caindo em desuso)
	I little bit. -> I speak English a little bit.
	Kind of.
	Not too bad.
	Not so much.

7.	Holy Moly!
	Oh my Gosh!
